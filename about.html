Mission and Vision:

At Start-up Healer, our team of seasoned experts brings extensive experience in consultancy, offering more than just services—we focus on building lasting relationships with our clients. We provide transparent startup support and personalized guidance, regardless of the business volume we receive. By prioritizing trust and client success, we not only gain appreciation but also long-term partnerships.

Our mission is clear: to create awareness about the benefits and schemes offered by state and central governments for every Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprise (MSME), all with a commitment to transparency and growth.

The Issue with Cheap-Rate Consultants:

In the era of emerging Indian entrepreneurs, thousands of start-ups are registered every month, often through CA/CS professionals who charge lower professional fees. This is because most new business owners are hesitant to invest heavily in professional services during the early stages. However, these consultants often exploit this mindset by providing only basic services at a cheap rate. At Start-up Healer, we recognize a crucial problem: many CA/CS professionals fail to create awareness about the complete process. They don't offer full transparency to their clients, especially about hidden clauses in government schemes or departmental processes. This lack of information can lead to avoidable mistakes, potentially landing start-ups in legal trouble. Why does this happen? Because if clients are fully aware of these clauses and processes, they are less likely to make mistakes—mistakes that these professionals rely on to charge additional fees. Instead of focusing on proactive support and proper guidance, their services revolve around reactive problem-solving. At Start-up Healer, we aim to change this narrative by empowering entrepreneurs with transparent, accurate, and actionable advice. We ensure our clients are informed and confident, helping them avoid costly errors while focusing on growing their businesses.

Why Choose Start-up Healer:

After serving thousands of clients across India, our founders identified critical gaps in the consultancy industry—lack of transparency, genuine guidance, practical commitments, startup support, and realistic expectations. This realization inspired them to establish Start-up Healer with the mission to bridge these gaps and redefine consultancy services.

Our team is committed to complete transparency because we believe that if our founders themselves sought the services we offer, they would expect proper guidance delivered with honesty. This belief aligns with the expectations of every client—after all, they are investing their hard-earned money.

Our transparent approach doesn’t just help us attract new clients; it also fosters trust and brings recurring business from our existing clients. At Start-up Healer, we aim to build lasting partnerships while providing unparalleled support to empower start-ups and businesses.
