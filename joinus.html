Application And Recruitment Process

1

Review Job Descriptions

Visit our Careers page on the company website and carefully
review the detailed job descriptions for the positions you are
interested in. Pay attention to the qualifications, responsibilities,
and requirements listed for each role.

2

Prepare Your Application

Prepare a comprehensive application package that includes
your updated resume, a tailored cover letter, and any other
relevant supporting documents such as portfolio samples or
certifications. Make sure your application highlights your
relevant skills, experiences, and enthusiasm for startups.

3

Submit Application

Apply from career page located on our website or Send your
application via email to hr@startupHealer.com - Use a clear
and professional subject line that includes the position title you
are applying for. Ensure that your application is well-formatted
and free of any errors.

4

Initial Screening

Our HR team will review all received applications and conduct
an initial screening based on the qualifications and
requirements outlined in the job descriptions. If you meet the
initial criteria, you will be shortlisted for further evaluation.

5

Interviews

If selected, you will be invited for interviews, which may include
multiple rounds. These interviews may be conducted in person,
via video conferencing, or over the phone. The interviews will
assess your skills, experiences, cultural fit, and passion for
startups. Be prepared to answer behavioral and technical questions
related to the position you applied for.

6

Assessments or Tests

Depending on the role, you may be required to complete
assessments, tests, or assignments to evaluate your skills and
capabilities. This may include technical tests, case studies, or
presentations. Further instructions will be provided during the
interview process.

7

Reference Checks

As a final step before making a hiring decision, we may
conduct reference checks with your previous employers or
professional contacts. Please ensure you have provided
accurate and up-to-date references.

8

Job Offer

If you successfully pass all stages of the selection process and
are selected as the top candidate, our HR team will extend a
job offer to you. The offer will include details such as
compensation, benefits, start date, and other relevant terms
and conditions.

9

Onboarding

Once you accept the job offer, our HR team will guide you
through the onboarding process, which may include
completing necessary paperwork, setting up your employee
accounts, and providing you with relevant information to help
you smoothly transition into your new role.
